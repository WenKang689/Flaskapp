Privacy Policy: Software Usage Data Collection for Auto-Recommendations

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This section of our Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information from your laptop when you opt-in to our auto-recommendation feature. By enabling this feature, you agree to the collection and use of your installed software data as outlined below.

2. What Information We Collect

When you choose to enable the auto-recommendation feature, our system may collect the following information from your laptop:

  • Installed Software Information: A list of software applications installed on your laptop, including version details and usage patterns.

3. How We Use the Collected Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Usage Detection: To analyze the types of software you use and how frequently they are used.
  • Personalized Recommendations: To provide tailored recommendations based on your software usage to enhance your productivity and system performance.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement industry-standard security measures to protect the information collected from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Data Sharing and Retention

  • Third-Party Sharing: We do not share your software usage data with any third parties unless required by law.
  • Data Retention: The data collected is retained only as long as necessary to provide you with the auto-recommendation service. Once the data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

6. Your Choices

  • Opting In: The collection of software usage data is optional and only occurs when you enable the auto-recommendation feature.
  • Opting Out: You can disable the auto-recommendation feature at any time through your account settings. Once disabled, no further data will be collected, and any previously collected data will be deleted.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated to you through our system or via email. Continued use of the auto-recommendation feature after any updates signifies your acceptance of the revised policy.
